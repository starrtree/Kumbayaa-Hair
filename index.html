Before You Book

Everything to know before your crown session.

Is hair included?

Hair inclusion varies by service and should be confirmed before booking. Use the notes field to request hair-included options.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

Please arrive detangled and prepared according to your selected service instructions unless a wash service is included.

How long do braid styles last?

Longevity depends on style, care routine, hair texture, and scalp needs. Your stylist can recommend a healthy wear window.

Do you service children?

Yes, kids protective styles are available with gentle styling and age-appropriate expectations.

Are deposits required?

Appointments may require a deposit. Final policy details can be added when the booking system is connected.
